## App name
**PROGNOS — Competitive Programming Tracker**

## Short description (Play, ≤80 chars)
Track your Codeforces progress, never miss a contest, compete with your class.

## Full description
PROGNOS is your competitive-programming companion — "Strava for CP." It turns your scattered
Codeforces activity into a clear picture of how you're improving, and makes sure you never miss a
round.

**Personal analytics**
- Activity heatmap, current & longest streaks, total solved
- Rating chart with per-contest deltas
- Tag breakdown, focus areas, and problem recommendations

**Contest radar**
- Every upcoming contest across major judges, in one place
- Live countdowns; list and week-calendar views
- On-device reminders that fire before a contest starts — offline, screen-off, reboot-proof

**Home-screen widget**
- Your next contest and current streak, at a glance

**Classrooms**
- Create or join a class, compare on a shared leaderboard
- Teachers get cohort analytics and invite management

Cached-first and offline-friendly: every screen you've seen renders instantly, even with no
connection.

## Privacy notes (for the data-safety form)
- Google Sign-In (auth only). Access token in memory; refresh token in the OS keystore.
- Codeforces handle + public submission stats, shown to classroom members you join.
- No ads, no third-party analytics SDKs.
- On-device reminder scheduling; no server push in v1.
